The Merritt Hunt Club

Nestled on the Connecticut Shoreline amid the scenic Norwalk Islands and harbor, we’re only a short distance from New York and Rhode Island harbors. As a non-profit recreational organization, we’re dedicated to providing a family-oriented, social environment and a sense of community. Dedicated to service, we strive for only the highest quality of service and facility, in a fiscally responsible manner.
